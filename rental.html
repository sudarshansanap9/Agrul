How AGRUL Rental Works

Custom Hiring Centres

TAFE's JFarm Services connects Custom Hiring Centres (CHCs) operated by tractor and equipment owners to farmers seeking farm mechanisation solutions

Subsidy and Benefits

Farmer-entrepreneurs (Partners) interested in establishing CHC in Rajasthan can avail subsidy from the government under the CHC scheme by partnering with TAFE. A 40% back-end subsidy can be availed from the Government of Rajasthan under the Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization (SMAM) guidelines.

The CHC owners and Partners have flexibility of deciding rental rates based on market conditions and other relevant factors.
